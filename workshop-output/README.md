# Workshop output

Create your workshop artifacts here:

- `PRODUCT-DIRECTION.md`
- `PRODUCT-MAP.md`
- `GOAL.md`
- `FEATURE_SPEC.md`
- `PLAN.md`
- `DECISIONS.md`
- `REVIEW.md`
- `CHANGE-WALKTHROUGH.md`
- `EVIDENCE/`

Start by copying `workshop/templates/PRODUCT-DIRECTION.md` here. Your product take and chosen direction come before the Goal, Spec and Plan.

The folder is intentionally empty at the start. Your artifact chain is the workshop deliverable.
